David Rach

I am a PhD Candidate in Molecular Microbiology and Immunology at the University of Maryland, School of Medicine. My thesis advisors are Drs. Cristiana Cairo and Kirsten Lyke.

I am a graduate of Northwest College where I received my A.S in Natural Resources Biology, and University of Wyoming where I received a B.S. in Molecular Biology and Microbiology.

It was doing undergraduate research in lab of Jason Gigley that I first became interested in Immunology.

Before moving to Baltimore for graduate school, I served in the Peace Corps as a Secondary Science teacher in Ghana from 2016-2018.
